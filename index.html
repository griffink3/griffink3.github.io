About

Hi! I'm Griffin Kao and I'm an Associate Product Manager at Google. With a background in computer science, mechanical engineering, and economics at Brown University, I've previously worked as a software engineer and product manager at companies both large and small, B2B and B2C. However, my most important role is as a human being. Empathy is my greatest strength and human affliction my greatest concern. Check out some of my work here!

In addition to my work, I also love writing, designing mechanical assemblies, early stage VC, and playing ultimate frisbee. In fact, two of my proudest achievements and memories are publishing my most recent book, Turning Silicon Into Gold, and becoming a D1 College National Champion!
